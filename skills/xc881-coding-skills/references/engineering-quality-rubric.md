# Rubric

Good: correct, scoped, traceable, simple, modular, testable, verified, honest.

Check: direct behavior, success check, boundary validation, explicit state/errors, visible security, one reason/module, infra at edges.

Bad: UI/SQL/callback business rules, circular deps, globals, God service, unstable contracts, swallowed errors, speculative abstraction, unrelated cleanup, unrequested flexibility.
